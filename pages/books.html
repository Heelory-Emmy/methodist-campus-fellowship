Frequently Asked Questions

What types of resources can I find here?

Our resource library includes Christian books, Bible study materials, academic resources, hymns and worship resources, past exam questions, and more. All resources are carefully selected to support your spiritual and academic growth.

How can I share my own resources?

Click the "Share a Resource" button at the top of the page or in the sharing section. You'll need to provide details about your resource and either upload a file or provide a link. All shared resources are reviewed before being added to the library.

Can I request specific resources?

Yes! If you need a specific resource that's not in our library, click the "Request a Resource" button at the bottom of the page. Our team will try to locate the resource or connect you with someone who can help.

How are resources verified?

All resources go through a review process by our fellowship team. We check for alignment with our values, content quality, and copyright compliance. This ensures that our library remains helpful, accurate, and legally sound.
