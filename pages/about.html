The Methodist Campus Fellowship (MCF) also known as the Abundant Life Family is a Campus Arm of the Youth Work Department of the Methodist Church Nigeria. Born out of deep passion of Christ, the Fellowship has become a veritable instrument for positive change in our campuses, our church and society at large.

The Fellowship is bent on preaching, teaching and living out the unadulterated gospel of Christ. Her goals and objectives are enshrined in her constitution. The Fellowship is opened to both Methodists and Non-Methodists students alike who have accepted the Lord as their Saviour and identify with the vision and mission of the Fellowship.

Vision and Mission

Vision

To revive the Wesleyan heritage and possess our generation for Christ.

Mission

  1. To create an avenue whereby Methodist students in every higher institution of learning in the country can come together as a christian body and have a constant fellowship in the lord.
  2. To labour together with other Christian fellowships on Campus to spread the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ.
  3. To build ourselves up into the measure of the fullness of Christ and to fulfil effectively our Lord's great commission "Go ye into all the world and preach the gospel to every creature" (Mark 16:15).
  4. To labour together by the help of God to accomplish the much needed revival in Methodist Church, Nigeria through the Fellowship's outreaches and collaboration with all the arms of the church.
  5. To stir ourselves up to our responsibilities to our father land.
